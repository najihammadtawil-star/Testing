Our Story

Built on Craft
and Commitment

Ironleaf Landscaping was founded on a straightforward belief: that residential outdoor spaces deserve the same level of care and intentionality as the homes they surround. Based in Katy, Texas, we serve homeowners across the greater Houston area who demand more than a quick fix - they want lasting transformation.

We are not a lawn maintenance company. We are an outdoor improvement firm. Every project we undertake is approached with precise planning, quality materials, and a design sensibility rooted in what makes a property exceptional. From the initial consultation to the final walk-through, we hold every detail to a higher standard.

Our team brings deep regional expertise in Texas soils, climate, and residential aesthetics - allowing us to deliver results that are not only beautiful but built to endure.

Our Services

Comprehensive outdoor improvement services for discerning homeowners across Greater Houston.

Landscape Design & Installation

Full landscape planning and installation for homeowners who want a polished, intentional exterior instead of a quick patchwork update.

Best For

Front yard curb appeal, backyard redesigns, and full property transformations.

Includes

Layout planning, plant selection, bed shaping, installation, and final detailing.

Result

A cohesive outdoor space built around your home, light, soil, and daily use.

Sod Installation & Turf Replacement

Premium sod installation and turf repair with preparation focused on clean edges, proper grading, and a lawn that establishes correctly in the Houston climate.

Best For

Patchy lawns, worn-out turf, new builds, and yards needing a fresh reset.

Includes

Old turf removal, ground prep, leveling, sod installation, and finish cleanup.

Result

A smoother, greener lawn with a stronger first impression from the street.

Mulch, Stone & Garden Bed Enhancements

Detail work that gives existing landscapes sharper shape, cleaner contrast, and better definition without requiring a complete redesign.

Best For

Tired beds, faded mulch, undefined edges, and planting areas that need structure.

Includes

Mulch, stone, edging, bed cleanup, plant refreshes, and finishing details.

Result

A cleaner, more refined property that looks maintained and intentional.

Property Cleanups, Refreshes & Trimming

One-time exterior cleanups and structural trimming for properties that need to be reset, prepared, or brought back into shape.

Best For

Seasonal overgrowth, listing preparation, event prep, and neglected outdoor spaces.

Includes

Leaf removal, shrub shaping, bed cleanup, debris removal, edging touchups, and outdoor refresh work.

Result

A sharper exterior that feels controlled, clean, and ready to be seen.

Drainage, Grading & Water Flow Solutions

Practical grading and drainage improvements that help protect your yard, beds, foundation edges, and usable outdoor areas from standing water.

Best For

Pooling water, soggy lawn areas, runoff problems, erosion, and low spots.

Includes

Site assessment, grading recommendations, drainage corrections, and water-flow improvements.

Result

A more reliable landscape that handles heavy rain with less damage and disruption.

Tree Services

Tree care and removal services for homeowners who need safer, cleaner, and more usable outdoor spaces.

Best For

Overgrown limbs, unsafe branches, unwanted trees, leftover stumps, and storm-related cleanup.

Includes

Tree trimming, tree removals, stump grinding, branch cleanup, debris removal, and property reset work.

Result

A cleaner property with improved safety, better clearance, and fewer obstacles in your outdoor space.

Exterior Cleaning

Professional exterior cleaning with powerwashing, softwashing, and window cleaning to restore curb appeal while using the right method for each surface.

Best For

Driveways, walkways, patios, fences, siding, entryways, windows, pool areas, and exterior surfaces with dirt, mildew, or buildup.

Includes

Powerwashing for durable hard surfaces, softwashing for more delicate areas, window cleaning, surface rinsing, spot attention, and cleanup after service.

Result

A cleaner, brighter property exterior with improved curb appeal and a more polished first impression.

General Outdoor Improvements & Upgrades

Custom exterior improvements for projects that need a skilled landscaping team but do not fit neatly into a single service category.

Best For

Small upgrades, finishing touches, repair-adjacent projects, seasonal installs, and custom property improvements.

Includes

Project planning, material coordination, installation work, Christmas light installs, and detailed finishing.

Result

A more complete outdoor space with the loose ends handled professionally.
